About PSX Hub

PSX Hub is a community-driven resource dedicated to keeping the PlayStation 1 and PlayStation 2 alive through modern modding, preservation, and education.

We believe these iconic consoles deserve to stay out of landfills and in the hands of gamers. Whether you're rediscovering classics on original hardware, backing up your collection, or exploring homebrew — we're here to help with clear, up-to-date guides and trusted tools.

From PS1 softmods like Tonyhax and FreePSXBoot to PS2 staples like FreeMcBoot and Open PS2 Loader, we cover accessible methods for all skill levels.

Built by enthusiasts, for enthusiasts — since 2026.
